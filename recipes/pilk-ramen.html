Pilk Ramen

The Pilk Ramen, a certified classic!

Easy to make, delicious, and light on the eyes, this recipe will impress your date!

Ingredients:

  • Instant ramen
  • Milk
  • Pepsi to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Add the contents of the flavor packets (you can add the whole flavour packets if you so wish)
  3. Start cooking the ramen in the milk
  4. Add the Pepsi
  5. Stir for a few seconds
  6. Serve
